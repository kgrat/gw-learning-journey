# Gravitational Wave Learning Journey 🌌

> Documenting a 12-week self-study journey into gravitational wave physics 
> and early universe cosmology, building towards research contributions 
> in next-generation GW astronomy (Cosmic Explorer / Einstein Telescope).

 
**Timeline:** May – July 2025  
**Focus:** Stochastic gravitational wave backgrounds, early universe signals, 
primordial phase transitions, cosmic strings

---

## Goal
To build the theoretical and computational foundation needed to contribute 
to science with next-generation gravitational wave detectors — specifically 
Cosmic Explorer and Einstein Telescope — with a focus on detecting 
primordial signals from the early universe.
